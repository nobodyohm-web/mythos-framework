# SOTA Research Cache — 2026-05-18 (deep-evolve v2)

## Theory (STEP A — arXiv / MIT)

- **`[E]` arXiv:2504.19678 v2 (Ferrag et al., Mar 2026)** — "From LLM Reasoning to Autonomous AI Agents: A Comprehensive Review". Surveys 60+ benchmarks 2019-2025 and AI-agent frameworks 2023-2025. Calls out **failure modes in multi-agent LLM systems** as an open problem. Surveys ACP / MCP / A2A inter-agent protocols.
  https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.19678

- **`[E]` MIT DSpace: "Stairway to Autonomy" (Nayak)** — Hierarchical decision-making for LLM-guided multi-agent navigation. Introduces **LLaMAR** (Language Model-based Long-Horizon Planner) with an explicit **plan-act-correct-verify** cycle. Mythos `skills/pacv.md` is the direct adaptation.
  https://dspace.mit.edu/handle/1721.1/162935

- **`[E]` arXiv:2601.12560v1 (Arunkumar, Gangadharan, Buyya — Jan 2026)** — "Agentic AI: Architectures, Taxonomies, and Evaluation of LLM Agents". Names three open challenges: **hallucination in action**, **infinite loops**, **prompt injection** — exactly the next three gaps Mythos closes in v5.2. Discusses Tree-of-Thoughts, ReAcTree, Reflexion as concrete cognitive architectures.
  https://arxiv.org/html/2601.12560v1

- **`[E]` arXiv:2512.02731v1 (Chojecki, Dec 2025)** — "Self-Improving AI Agents through Self-Play". Defines the **Generator-Verifier-Updater (GVU) operator** as a moduli-theoretic primitive, proves the **Variance Inequality** as sufficient condition for `κ > 0` (positive self-improvement rate). Mythos `bin/mythos-gvu` is the operational realization.
  https://arxiv.org/html/2512.02731v1
